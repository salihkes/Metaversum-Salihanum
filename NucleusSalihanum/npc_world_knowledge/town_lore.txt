The town has four main landmarks: the Market, Town Center, Park, and BuildingA.

The Market is where merchants sell goods. It is located to the east of town. A guard patrols the Market entrance.

Town Center is the heart of the town where people gather. Janet can often be found here greeting visitors.

The Park is a peaceful green area to the west. It is a good place to relax.

BuildingA is a large structure used for official business. It is north of the Market.

The town has sidewalks connecting all major areas. People are encouraged to walk on the sidewalks.

The weather changes throughout the day. When it rains, people should seek shelter in buildings.

The town operates on a daily schedule. The guard reports to the Market at 9:00 and stays until evening.
