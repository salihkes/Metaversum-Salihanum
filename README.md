# Metaversum-Salihanum

A personal metaverse foundation built with Godot Engine and Python, providing a ready-to-use foundation for anyone who wants to create their own digital space.

## Origin Story

The passion for creating a personal digital space (now commonly called a metaverse) originates in December 2009 when I (salih1, known as salihkes on Github) discovered it was possible to create your own experiences within the ROBLOX platform. Having built multiple experiences with immersive worlds and lore behind them—most notably Polandball Roleplay—I couldn't stop there. I wanted to build my own digital space where people could enter through any method, whether on a standard computer or through virtual reality.

This changed in 2024. ROBLOX was banned in Turkey in August 2024, and my exile from various Discord ROBLOX communities from 2020 to 2024 didn't help my situation either.

It was time to act. I had already made various Godot projects before, such as OpenPolandballRoleplay. But now it felt like an existential crisis—I had literally no digital representation anymore.

This project aims to not only fix that by providing a foundation to build upon, but potentially help you as well. My goal is simple: finish the project, release the game on a dedicated website, and release the entire code under AGPL v3 on Github.

**Target Release Date**: I was planning to release this project on September 19, 2025 (the 16th anniversary of me joining ROBLOX) as that day was significant to me. But the day has passed and currently the new release date is unknown.

This project will let my digital presence flourish where my community and I uphold each other, rather than repeating many of the mistakes I made in Polandball Roleplay.

I want this project to help you potentially achieve this as well, with the same quality. This is one of many reasons why I am open-sourcing the code—humanity must uphold and nurture each other.

## What You Can Do

### Express Yourself Through Characters
- **Humanoid Avatar**: A traditional avatar system with support for numerous accessories and custom textures
- **Countryball Character**: Spherical characters with emotions and animations, similar to ROBLOX Countryball games. Express emotions like happy, sad, serious, or neutral
- **Switch Freely**: Transform between character types anytime, and your choice is remembered across sessions
- **Upload Custom Textures**: Personalize your character through the web interface

### Experience Immersive Worlds
- **Dynamic Content Delivery**: Maps and assets are downloaded on demand from the server as versioned PCK packages, keeping the base game small. Only changed packages are re-downloaded
- **Spherical Planet Gameplay**: Play on a spherical planet with authentic gravity mechanics that affect movement and physics
- **Multiple Planets**: Explore different planets, each with their own gravity
- **Full VR Support**: Experience everything in virtual reality with complete OpenXR integration, or play traditionally on desktop
- **Spatial Voice Chat**: Talk to others with 3D positional audio—voices come from where players actually are in the world
- **Dynamic Environment**: Custom skies, water effects, rain, atmospheric lighting, and day/night cycles create a living world

### Own Your Space
- **Personal Plots**: Own land in the metaverse to express yourself
- **Furniture & Decoration**: Place and arrange objects in your plot to make it your own
- **Teleportation**: Quickly travel to locations, public areas, or friends' plots

### Conquer the Province Map
- **Claim Territory**: Click unowned provinces on the world map to make them yours, painted in your auto-assigned colour
- **Occupy Rivals**: Click another player's province to occupy it—your colour overlays theirs
- **Defend Your Land**: Click your own occupied province to kick out the occupier
- **Unclaim Provinces**: Click your own unoccupied province to release it
- **Peace Treaties**: Shift+Click an occupied province to propose a two-sided peace treaty with the other player; they get a popup with Accept/Reject and a 30-second countdown. Accepted treaties transfer all mutually occupied provinces to their occupiers
- **Persistent Map State**: Every change is replicated in real-time to all players and saved on the server

### Share Experiences Together
- **Live Media Streaming**: Watch videos, movies, or live streams together in-world
- **Shared Viewing**: Host movie nights, watch sports events, or share presentations with friends
- **Real-time Multiplayer**: See and interact with other players as they move through the world

### Build & Create (Offline Studio)
An offline building system inspired by ROBLOX Studio, allowing creative freedom without platform dependency:
- Select, move, resize, and rotate objects with intuitive tools
- Paint with colors and apply materials
- Create and organize objects into groups
- Full keyboard shortcuts: copy, paste, cut, duplicate, undo/redo
- Save and load your workspace locally
- Export your creations to OBJ format for use in tools like Blender
- All building works completely offline—true creative independence

### Access Anywhere
- **Web Launcher**: Log in, customize your character, and launch the game from your browser
- **Guest Mode**: Try it out without creating an account
- **Remote Play**: Stream the game to any device with a web browser
